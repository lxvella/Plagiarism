Computer Security Concepts

What is a Computer Virus?

Computer viruses are a type of malware that spread by "infecting" other files on a disk or computer. The first virus, called "Brain," appeared in 1986 and spread through pirated software.

- AO Kaspersky Lab (2024)

Malicious Software (Malware)

Malware refers to any program designed to perform harmful actions on systems. Cybercriminals use it to steal data, extort money, or cause damage.

- Myra Security GmbH (2024)

Spam Messages

Email spam refers to unsolicited messages, often sent in bulk. Botnets, controlled by cybercriminals, are often used to distribute spam.

- TechTarget (2024)
